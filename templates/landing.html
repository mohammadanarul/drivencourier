FAQ's

Q1. Why would I need courier service?

Courier service is often used for business purposes, as it is a fast and reliable way to send documents and other items. It can also be used for personal reasons, such as sending a birthday present to a friend.

Q2. What does it cost for a courier?

It depends on the size and weight of the package, as well as the distance it needs to travel.

Q3. How quickly can you deliver my package?

It depends on the shipping method you select. Our standard shipping method is UPS Ground and takes 1-5 business days.

Q4. When will my order be shipped?

You should receive a confirmation email with your order number when you place your order. If you can't find the email, please check your spam folder. If you still can't find your order number, please email us at hello@wanderlustcph.com with your full name and the email address you used to place your order and we'll send it to you.

Q5. Can I change my delivery address?

If you are looking to change your delivery address after placing your order, please contact our customer service team at www.dealdash.com/customerservice. Please note that we are unable to change your delivery address after you have won an item.

Once you have won an item, the deal is considered closed. If you have won an item, but have not received it, please contact our customer support team at www.dealdash.com/customerservice.

Can I pay for shipping?

If you are looking to pay for shipping, please click on the shipping tab in the listing. If the shipping tab is not available, the seller is unable to ship to your location.
